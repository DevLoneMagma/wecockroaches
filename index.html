2026 The Day The Movement Was Born

"Unemployed youth who cannot find jobs — they run to media, RTI activism, and attack the system. Like cockroaches."

— Chief Justice of India, Supreme Court, May 15 2026

जज साहब ने सोचा था हमें crush कर देंगे।
हमने सोचा — चलो, party बनाते हैं।

On May 15, 2026, from the highest bench in the country, the Chief Justice of India compared unemployed youth — students, teachers, activists — to cockroaches. His point was that job-seekers who turn to RTI activism and media are a nuisance to the system.

He was right about one thing: cockroaches are a nuisance to the system. They survive nuclear winters. They live in the walls of every VIP bungalow. They cannot be exterminated. They multiply.

So we took the insult. We wore it. We built the Cockroach Janta Party — not a registered political party, but a movement of every student, teacher, unemployed youth, RTI activist, journalist, and citizen the system has tried to crush.

यह movement है। Party तो बस शुरुआत है।

Graduation degree in hand. No job in sight. Government solution: fry pakodas. Next policy update will classify begging as a "gig economy opportunity" and assign you a GSTIN for receiving alms.

"Rozgar chahiye toh skill develop karo. Jugaad hi asli innovation hai bhai."

#ElectoralBonds
Legalized Chanda घूस, पर legal वाली

Brilliant scheme: corporations donate unlimited money to parties anonymously. Totally not suspicious. Absolutely no quid pro quo. The Supreme Court agreed it was unconstitutional. The money is already spent. Oops.

"Scam nahi tha — donation tha. Difference samjho. Desh ke liye sacrifice."

#Judiciary
Retire Ho Rajya Sabha Jao न्याय की कीमत — एक seat

Retire as Chief Justice on Monday. Accept Rajya Sabha nomination on Tuesday. This is called "judicial independence." The robe changes; the party card stays the same. Perfectly normal in the world's largest democracy.

"Insult toh insult hai — chahe bench se aaye ya box se."

#Media
Godi Media Supremacy टीआरपी > सच्चाई

Breaking news at 9PM: Anchor in studio discovers ancient Hindu temple under journalist's spine. Opposition questioned. Nation demands to know. Actual news — farmer deaths, factory fires, bank scams — happens at 3AM with no anchor.

"Desh jaanna chahta hai — lekin sirf woh jo channel ko suit kare."

#Infrastructure
Bridge Gaya Re Tender बना, Bridge गिरा

Inaugurated on Monday with red ribbon. Collapsed on Wednesday with full irony. Investigation launched on Thursday. Tender awarded to minister's nephew's company on Friday. By Saturday, a new bridge is already being "planned."

"Quality check toh tha — bas contractor ka phone switch off tha us waqt."

Our Demands

Manifesto of the
Unkillable जो नहीं टूटते उनकी माँगें

01

No Post-Retirement Rajya Sabha

CJI retire हों तो घर जाएँ, Rajya Sabha नहीं

Any Chief Justice who retires shall not be eligible for any political appointment for 10 years. Judiciary independence means actual independence — not independence until the party needs a loyal vote in the upper house.

Non-Negotiable
02

Every Deleted Vote = Criminal Act

एक भी vote delete हुआ तो Election Commissioner जेल जाएगा

Voter roll deletions must be treated as electoral fraud. Any systematic deletion of legitimate voters from electoral rolls shall be prosecuted under the strictest provisions of the law. Voting is not a privilege. It is a right.

Constitutional
03

50% Women. Not 33. Fifty.

Parliament में 50% महिलाएँ — negotiation बंद

We don't want a third of democracy for half the population. We want half. 50% reservation in Parliament. 50% in Cabinet. Because representation is not charity — it is justice.

Zero Compromise
04

Audit Every Godi Anchor

जो सच नहीं बोलता, उसके bank account की जाँच हो

Media houses receiving political funding while claiming editorial independence shall be publicly audited. Anchors who run prime-time propaganda while calling it journalism should answer one simple question: who is paying for the studio?

Press Freedom
05

Anti-Defection for 20 Years

Party छोड़ी तो 20 साल के लिए राजनीति से बाहर

Any elected MLA or MP who defects to another party loses the right to contest any election or hold any public office for 20 years. You were elected on a mandate — not a temp contract. Honor it or leave politics entirely.

Anti-Corruption
06

RTI is Sacred. Not a Nuisance.

RTI कार्यकर्ता hero हैं — cockroach नहीं

The Right to Information is a constitutional tool for accountability. Any official who delays, denies, or obstructs an RTI application shall face mandatory disciplinary action within 30 days. Sunlight is the best disinfectant. Even for cockroaches.

Hall of
Shame वो कारनामे जो भूलने नहीं देंगे

Verdict: Disaster SHAME / 001
Demonetization रात 8 बजे की वो announcement

8PM announcement. ATMs ran dry for months. 100+ people died standing in queues. ₹99.3% of banned currency returned to banks. Mission: eliminate black money. Result: eliminate poor people's savings. Black money slept in gold and benami property. As always.

"Kuch dino ki takleef hai — desh ke liye sacrifice karo." They said. We lined up. We sacrificed. The black money did not.

Verdict: Surveillance SHAME / 002
Pegasus Snooping फ़ोन में जासूस बैठा था

Military-grade spyware found on phones of journalists, opposition leaders, judges, and activists. Government response: neither confirm nor deny. Parliament: disrupted. Investigation: none. The surveillance state watched you complain about the surveillance state.

"Agar kuch galat nahi kiya toh darr kya hai?" They watched everything. Found nothing. Kept watching.

Verdict: Unconstitutional SHAME / 003
Electoral Bonds legal घूस की scheme

Scheme where corporations could donate unlimited anonymous money to political parties. Uniquely, the purchasing bank (SBI — government-owned) knew who bought what. The government knew. The donors knew. Only the voters didn't. Supreme Court called it unconstitutional. The money was already spent.

"Transparency hai — bas aapke liye nahi." For 7 years this was the law of the land.

Verdict: Preventable SHAME / 004
The Bridge Files नींव भ्रष्टाचार, छत उद्घाटन

Morbi bridge, Machar bridge, Mizoram rail bridge — India leads the world in bridges that collapse shortly after inauguration. Every collapse: tender irregularities. Every investigation: stalled. Every contractor: connected. The engineering marvel is that anyone crosses any bridge with confidence.

"Quality assured tha. CCTV footage available nahi hai." Justice is also unavailable.
