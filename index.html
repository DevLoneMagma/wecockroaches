Exhibit C · Our Demands

The
Demands

जो नहीं टूटते — उनकी माँगें।

01

No Post-Retirement Rajya Sabha

CJI retire हों — घर जाएँ। Rajya Sabha नहीं।

Any Chief Justice who retires is ineligible for political appointment for 10 years. Judicial independence means actual independence. Not independence until the party needs a reliable vote upstairs.

02

One Deleted Vote = Criminal Act

एक भी vote delete — Election Commission जवाब देगा।

Systematic deletion of voters from electoral rolls is prosecuted as fraud. No "technical glitch." No "database error." A vote is a constitutional right. Erasing it is a constitutional crime.

03

50% Women. Not 33. Fifty.

आधी आबादी को आधा हक। पूरा।

Half the population will not accept a third of democracy. 50% in Parliament. 50% in Cabinet. Representation is not charity.

04

Audit Every Prime Time Anchor

जो सच न बोले — उसका account खुले।

Media houses receiving political funding while claiming editorial independence face mandatory public audit. One question: who is paying for the studio, and what are they getting back?

05

Defect Once. Out for 20 Years.

Party छोड़ी तो 20 साल के लिए — बाहर।

Any MLA or MP who switches parties loses the right to hold public office for 20 years. You were elected on a mandate. Honor it or return the seat.

06

RTI is a Right. File It. Protect It.

RTI activist hero हैं। Cockroach नहीं।

Every RTI answered within 30 days. Obstruction carries mandatory criminal penalties. The right to ask questions is not a nuisance. It's democracy working correctly.
